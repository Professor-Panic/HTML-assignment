My anime list

Anime Ranking Summary
One Piece 8.8/10 One Piece is a manga and anime series by Eiichiro Oda that follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young man with rubber-like powers gained from eating a Devil Fruit, as he sails the seas to find the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" and become the King of the Pirates.
Demon Slayer 8.8/10 Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy in Taishō-era Japan whose family is slaughtered by the demon progenitor Muzan Kibutsuji, leaving only his sister Nezuko alive but transformed into a demon. Unlike other demons, Nezuko retains her humanity and resists eating humans, prompting Tanjiro to join the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and find a cure for her.
Attack on Titan 8.8/10 Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy series by Hajime Isayama centered on Eren Yeager, who vows to destroy the Titans—giant, man-eating humanoids—after they breach the outer wall and kill his mother. Humanity survives within three concentric walls (Maria, Rose, and Sina), using Vertical Maneuvering Equipment to combat these creatures, while Eren and his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert join the military to uncover the truth behind the Titans.
Naruto 3.8/10 Naruto Uzumaki, an orphan shunned by his village for housing the Nine-Tailed Fox, dreams of becoming Hokage to earn respect. He joins Team 7 with rivals Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, facing threats like rogue ninja Orochimaru and the criminal organization Akatsuki.
Bleach 4.8/10 Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who becomes a Substitute Soul Reaper after absorbing powers from Rukia Kuchiki to protect his family from evil spirits called Hollows. His journey takes him from defending Karakura Town to infiltrating the Soul Society to rescue Rukia from execution, where he uncovers the deception of Sousuke Aizen.
